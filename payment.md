 Client → Hello Team (Collecting payment)                                                                                                                                                      
  - Client pays for the service (invoices)                                                                                                - Uses whatever method the client chose (ACH, card, etc.)
  - This is collecting money IN

  Hello Team → Employee (Paying salary)

  - Hello Team pays employees their wages
  - This is sending money OUT (payroll payout)
  - Employee needs to provide their bank account or debit card to receive funds

  So for employees:

  The employee needs to set up how they want to get paid. Options:

  1. ACH (bank transfer) — employee provides bank account + routing number
  2. Debit card push — employee provides their debit card

  We should let the employee choose their payout method and enter their details in their profile.

  Suggested flow:

  1. Employee goes to profile/settings → "Payment Method"
  2. Chooses ACH or Debit Card
  3. Enters their details (bank info or card)
  4. When admin finalizes payroll and clicks "Pay" → Sola sends money to each employee via their chosen method
